The ashen sky seemed to burthen the earth as the earthen pottery shattered into pieces.

The heathen group was shocked to find that the freshened spring water had turned the hen into a golden color.

As the day lengthened, the lichen on the rocks began to glow and the moorhen swimming in the pond seemed to be having a grand time.

The peahen watched with interest as the young bird tried to strengthen and toughen its wings by flapping them vigorously.
