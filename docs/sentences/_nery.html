Last month, the local deanery was bewildered to find that the nearby cannery had been shut down due to extensive chicanery, leaving the town without its beloved confectionery.

The joinery was a sight to behold, as its intricate finery combined perfectly with the lush greenery of the surrounding countryside; however, nearby the sound of gunnery arms tests echoed over the horizon.

The nunnery had difficulty maintaining their machinery due to the ornery demeanor of the new apprentice, who seemed far more interested in browsing the millinery than doing any real work.

The scenery of the coast was breathtaking, with its glittering refinery, lush winery fields, and bustling tannery all adding to the charm of the area.
