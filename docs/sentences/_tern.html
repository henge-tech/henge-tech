The bittern flew a stern flight over the cistern, flapping its wings with determination and skill unlike any other creature in the world.

Interns at the office were often asked to head outside to fetch a cittern from the extern building, and many enjoyed the task as it gave them a chance to get some fresh air.

The intricate pattern on the old lantern was echoed, though in a more muted form, on the lectern in the corner of the room, creating an interesting visual harmony.

The stern captain barked orders at the subalterns, who sprang into action to follow his commands as the terns circled overhead.
