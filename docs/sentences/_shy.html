As the bushy tree swayed in the breeze, it created a cool and cushy feeling that made me feel ashy and dishy all at once.

The marshy area was full of life, with fleshy plants and fishy creatures swimming around in the flashy sunlight.

The snow was slushy and mushy as I walked along the path, feeling a bit shy as the wind started to get pushy.

The waves of the ocean lapped against the shore, creating a splashy and squishy sound as the tushy seagulls flew over the trashy beach.
