The brainy, briny waters of destiny beckoned him forward, informing him that his future would be both extraordinary and unpredictable.

He felt a deep sense of grainy hominy and ignominy fill the air, signifying that he was doomed to a life of shame and humiliation.

When the crew of the ship decided to mutiny against their captain, they were met with a great deal of scrutiny and shiny glares of disapproval from their peers.

The spiny tentacles of villainy wrapped around him, radiating a whiny hum that made his skin crawl with dread.
