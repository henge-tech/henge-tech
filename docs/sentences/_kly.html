She stared blankly out of the window, her thoughts bleakly reflecting the biweekly routine of her life.

Frankly speaking, she walked briskly and meekly down the street, uncertain of where her journey would take her.

The starkly bright sun revealed a landscape of prickly cacti and sickly yellow grass, creating an unforgiving atmosphere.

She had aged quickly, her skin becoming wrinkly and weakly covered in a layer of thickly applied makeup.
