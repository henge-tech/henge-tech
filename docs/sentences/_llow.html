The bellow of the wind could be heard, billowing and callowing through the trees as the disallowed sound of the town allowed a peaceful atmosphere.

The hallowed ground was filled with a sense of solemnity as the fallow fields stretched beyond the horizon, fellows following in the footsteps of their leader, their hollow voices echoing through the air.

Mellow light filtered through the window, casting a sallow hue on the wall and the pillow on which a schoolfellow was peacefully sleeping, a gentle breeze rustling through the mallow trees outside.

Swallow birds twittered in the willow trees while their reflections moved slowly in the shallow lake, creating ripples that disturbed the wallow of a small creature covered in tallow, making them scurry away.
