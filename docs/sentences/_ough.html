An enterprising baker in a small borough kneaded his dough in the bough of an old maple tree, dreaming of the delicious baked goods he would craft to delight the townspeople.

As the weary soldier took his final furlough before enlisting in the army, he ventured to the lough shore and felt the rough winds of change blowing gently against his face.

The young artisan stirred the sourdough with enthusiasm, ensuring that it was mixed thoroughly before baking it in the oven at the edge of the slough; the end result was a perfect loaf of bread.

Though he had only a short amount of time, he managed to make it through the trickiest of tasks and fill the trough with food, ready for the animals to enjoy.
