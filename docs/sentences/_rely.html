The woman walked demurely and austerely through the bizarrely decorated room, barely able to contain her excitement.

He leisurely strolled through the park, merely admiring the beauty around him, unaware of the storm that was coming prematurely and purely to disrupt his peace.

She rarely relied on anyone for security, severely underestimating her worth and believing she could only rely on herself to remain securely afloat.

He looked squarely at the person standing before him and spoke sincerely, even though it pained him sorely to do so, as he was sure he would lose in the end.
