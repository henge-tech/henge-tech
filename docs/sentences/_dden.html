After years of being bedridden and downtrodden, he felt as if all his hopes were forbidden and bidden away from his life.

Even though he was hidden in the midden, his heart was gladden when he found out his dreams were not completely madden by life's difficulties.

Even though he had been ridden with sorrow and fears, and his heart was heavily sadden, his will to succeed was never overridden by a sense of despair that turned his cheeks to redden.

As his footsteps were suddenly trodden onto the sodden ground, a wave of unbidden emotion overwhelmed him and for a moment the world seemed to stand still.
