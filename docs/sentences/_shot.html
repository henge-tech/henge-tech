Jack awoke to a bloodshot sky, the buckshot echoing through the air as he heard someone shouting in earshot down the street.

The gunshot rang out in the night, and the hotshot quickly reloaded while his mailshot missed its target wide by several feet.

His mugshot stared out from the wanted flyer, and despite his best efforts to overshoot the police, his potshot missed the mark.

He fired off a scattershot of bullets and quickly reloaded his slingshot, but the upshot of his plan was a dismal failure.
