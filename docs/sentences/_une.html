At the bottom of a massive dune, a small commune of people with autoimmune diseases gathered to share resources, support one another, and live in harmony with nature.

At an inopportune moment, a desperate person importuned another for help, but unfortunately their immune system was unable to provide assistance.

Despite her picayune circumstance, the young woman was fortunate to meet someone at the opportune moment that could help her overcome her misfortune.

The tribal tribune carved a protective rune into the bark of the ancient prune tree, ensuring that his people would remain safe.
