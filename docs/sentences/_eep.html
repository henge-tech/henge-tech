The roosters around the barnyard started to bleep as the sun began to rise, and soon the sleepy chickens were cheeping as they slowly awoke from their slumber.

The dampness of the morning air filled the meadow with a seeping mist as the small creatures crept out of their dens, while a few birds peeped from the branches of tall trees.

The fog was rolled up like a blanket over the steep hills as the shepherd herded his flock of sheep, their woolly coats swishing together with each sweep of his crook.

As the veep of the company gave his speech, many in the audience kept up their murmured conversations while a few people wept at the powerful words he spoke about the importance of upkeep and maintenance.
