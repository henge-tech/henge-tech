Coincidentally, a fratricide occurred when two brothers used fungicides to fight against each other, showing how quickly peaceful tools can turn into disastrous weapons.

It is horrifying to think that something as seemingly harmless as an herbicide can be used to commit genocide or even more atrocious acts such as homicide.

It is truly heartbreaking that infanticide, insecticide and matricide still exist in our world today, as these cruel terms imply the merciless taking of innocent lives.

Patricide, pesticide, and suicide are all deeply tragic forms of violence that show the darkest depths of humanity; yet unfortunately, these tragedies happen far too often.
