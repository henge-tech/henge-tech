During the era of antislavery, it took an immense amount of bravery and dedication to ensure the safe delivery of those facing slavery in the Underground Railroad.

Through the process of discovery, it became apparent that wearing a livery uniform gave hope to those struggling to recover from an oppressive environment.

It was a moment of rediscovery when the silvery moonlight shone down on the shivery night, bringing a sense of liberation.

The unfortunate reality of slavery drove some to pursue thievery as the only way to a very limited form of freedom.
