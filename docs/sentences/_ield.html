The rolling hills of green fields quickly changed to a landscape of brownfield and coalfield as we drove through the quaint town of chesterfield, where the locals were busy preparing for an upcoming festival.

The infield and midfield of this abandoned goldfield were a reminder of how quickly wealth and fortune could be forgotten, leaving behind only a few lonely relics of its former glory.

As the sun beat down on the outfield, a figure clad in shining armor slowly emerged, wielding a mighty sword and calmly awaiting the enemy to yield and accept defeat.
