_pen

A cheapen aspen fell into the bullpen and stirred up a commotion, as its presence certainly did not belong there and was clearly out of place.

The lumpen hempen cloth had been dampened over and over, yet it still retained its vigor and its resilience, a testament to its strength of construction and quality.

As the misshapen pigpen was finally transformed into a beautiful playpen, it felt like the light of hope had cascaded down and illuminated the area with its warmth and joy.

As the fruits ripened to perfection, the farmer was ready to reopen his farm stand, and with a few deft strokes of a knife, he sharpened his produce to perfection.
